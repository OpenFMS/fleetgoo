标题：D501 3 channel 1080P Dashcam AI

描述：
Designed for commercial vehicles (compliant with Chinese Ministry standards,ride-hailing,logistics),this Al dash cam integrates dual 1080P video,cloud storage, BeiDou positioning,Driver Monitoring System (DMS),and Advanced Driver Assistance System(ADAS)to enhance safety and fleet management efficiency

核心功能
HD Video：Dual 1080P H.265 encoding, TS stream format; front camera (123° FOV) + rear AHD camera (140° ultra-wide angle) with night vision
Intelligent Safety DMS：Detects fatigue (yawning, eye-closing), smoking, calling, and distraction
Intelligent Safety ADAS：Alerts for lane departure, forward collision, pedestrian detection
Cloud Management：Critical video cloud storage, remote live streaming, snapshot capture, and route replay with geofencing
Stable Power：9-36V wide voltage input, short-circuit/reverse polarity protection, self-recovery fuse
Protocol Compliance：Supports JT/T 794/808/1076/1078 standards, remote configuration, and fault detection
Extended Functions：In-vehicle WiFi hotspot, OTA updates, TTS voice alerts, parking vibration wake-up monitoring

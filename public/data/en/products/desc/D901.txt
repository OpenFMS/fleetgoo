标题：D901 4-Channel Truck Video Recording All-in-One Device

描述：Compact all-in-one device with central control screen for freight and logistics monitoring. Supports 4-channel AHD video input, AI blind spot warnings, sentry mode, fuel theft prevention alerts, AI voice recognition, and 4G remote monitoring

核心功能
Multi-Channel Video：4-channel AHD video input, simultaneous recording in H.264 format (saved separately per channel)
AI Detection & Alerts：Built-in AI algorithms for blind spot warnings, fuel theft prevention, and voice recognition
Sentry Mode：Enhanced security monitoring mode
Remote Monitoring：4G connectivity enables remote live view, playback, track viewing, electronic fence, fleet management
Flexible Display：10.1-inch IPS touchscreen; default 4-screen display, supports switching to single-channel full screen; automatic view switching upon turn signal trigger
Stable Power：12V-36V wide voltage input, compliant with automotive-grade surge, overload, over-voltage, under-voltage, and short-circuit protection circuits

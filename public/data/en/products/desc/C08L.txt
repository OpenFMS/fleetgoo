标题：C08L Wired Locator

描述：
Designed for asset risk control,integrates GPS/BeiDou/ GLONASS triple-mode positioning with low power consumption,real-time monitoring,and remote control. Ideal for cargo tracking and vehicle security

Low Power：Auto-sleep mode, vibration-triggered activation
Security：Remote fuel/power cutoff, anti-tamper alerts, anti-fake base station detection
Robust Design：Anti-removal SIM slot, dual hardware/software protection, defect rate <3‰
Customization：JT808 protocol compatibility, supports private customization
Remote Management：OTA updates, SMS/APP configuration, real-time status analysis

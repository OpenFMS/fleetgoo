# FleetGoo Fleet Management Platform - Core Capabilities

## Platform Overview
- **Name**: FleetGoo Intelligent Fleet Management Platform
- **Type**: Enterprise-grade SaaS + Mobile App Ecosystem
- **Core Value**: Integrates real-time GPS tracking, video telematics, and AI safety analytics into a single unified dashboard.
- **Accessibility**: Web Portal (Command Center), Mobile Apps (iOS/Android), and Open API.

## Core Features (Operations Dashboard)
- **Real-Time GPS Location**: 
    - Obtain real-time vehicle location and status (speed, ignition, mileage, driving time) with sub-second latency.
    - Visualize entire fleet on Google Maps/OpenStreetMap with clustered markers.
- **Operations Dashboard**: 
    - Comprehensive visualization of operations, safety scores, and data trends.
    - At-a-glance metrics for fleet availability, utilization, and maintenance status.
- **Video Surveillance (CMS)**: 
    - Support for live streaming from up to 8 channels per vehicle simultaneously.
    - Remote snapshot capture, two-way audio intercom, and PTZ control.
    - View multiple vehicles in grid view (4/9/16 split screens).
- **Video & Track Playback**: 
    - Historical route replay with speed curves, stops, and alarm event markers.
    - Synchronized video playback: Watch recorded footage alongside the vehicle's historical position on the map.
    - Remote video download from device SD card/HDD to local system.
- **Remote Control Command**: 
    - Cut off engine/fuel remotely (for anti-theft).
    - Remote parameter configuration (OTA updates) for devices.

## AI Safety & Analytics (ADAS/DMS)
- **Safety Reporting**: 
    - Aggregated driver behavior scores based on harsh braking, rapid acceleration, and sharp turns.
    - AI event categorization: Fatigue, Distraction, Phone Use, Smoking, Lane Departure, Forward Collision.
- **Evidence Management**:
    - Automatic upload of event clips (alarm videos) to the cloud.
    - Evidence lock prevents critical footage from being overwritten.

## Operational Control
- **Geo-Fencing**: 
    - Create custom polygon, circular, or route-based geofences.
    - Trigger instant alerts via Email/SMS/App Push when vehicles enter or exit valid zones.
- **Fuel Monitoring**:
    - High-precision fuel level charts (requires ultrasonic/capacitive sensor).
    - Abrupt fuel drop alerts (theft protection).
- **Maintenance Module**:
    - Scheduling based on mileage, engine hours, or time intervals.
